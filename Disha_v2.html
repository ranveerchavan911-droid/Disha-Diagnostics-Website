Advanced Procedures

Specialised Services & Evaluations

Fetal Invasive Procedures

Performed by experienced specialists using precision guidance — high-risk procedures offered in-house for patient convenience and safety.

  • Chorionic Villous Sampling (CVS) — 10 to 14 weeks
  • Amniocentesis — after 16 weeks
  • Fetal Reduction
  • Fetal Interstitial Laser Therapy (RFA)
  • Fetal Cordocentesis

Fetal Genetic Evaluation

Comprehensive genomic and biochemical testing to support informed antenatal decision-making and counselling.

  • Double Marker — 11 to 14 weeks
  • Quadruple Marker Test — 15 to 20 weeks
  • NIPT (after NT scan)
  • FISH and Karyotyping
  • Microarray and Clinical Exome Sequencing
  • Antenatal Counselling

Third Trimester Monitoring

Detailed assessment of fetal health, development, and well-being beyond 24 weeks with advanced imaging modalities.

  • Fetal Growth and Well-being Scan
  • Fetal Neurosonography
  • Obstetrics Doppler
  • 3D / 4D Sonography
  • Cervix Elastography
  • Fetal Echo (20–24 weeks)

USG Guided Procedures

  • USG Guided Abscess Drainage

Technology Spotlight

Shear Wave Elastography (SWE)

Carpal Tunnel Syndrome — Beyond Conventional Ultrasound

Quantitative Median Nerve Stiffness Assessment

Shear Wave Elastography measures median nerve stiffness in kPa — detecting functional compression earlier and more reliably than cross-sectional area measurement alone.

  • Operator-independent and highly reproducible measurement
  • Correlates directly with electrodiagnostic severity grading
  • Outperforms CSA alone with 93.3% sensitivity
  • Ideal for borderline CSA cases and pre-surgical planning
  • Non-invasive — no discomfort, no nerve conduction required
